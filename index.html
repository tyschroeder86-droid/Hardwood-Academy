Why Hardwood Academy

Not a camp.
A commitment.

Most youth basketball programs shuffle kids through group drills where they blend into the crowd and never get the focused instruction they need. That's not how real improvement happens.

At Hardwood Academy, every single session is built around your player — their strengths, their weak spots, their goals. No templates. No cookie-cutter curricula. Just deliberate, high-rep skill work that translates directly to game performance.

Founded and coached by Ty Schroeder, Hardwood Academy has one mission: develop young players the right way so they step onto the court with confidence, skill, and love for the game.
