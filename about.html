Coaching Philosophy

What I believe.

01
Fundamentals First
Every great player is built on the basics. We don't skip steps — we master them. Ball handling, footwork, and form come before everything else.
02
No Kid Gets Left Behind
Every session is tailored to the individual. We meet players where they are — not where we wish they were. Beginners and advanced players both get what they need.
03
Fun Is Non-Negotiable
Kids play better when they're enjoying themselves. High energy, low pressure, meaningful work. The best training sessions don't feel like training.
The Story

Why I started
Hardwood Academy.

I created Hardwood Academy because I believe every kid deserves coaching that actually sees them as an individual — not just a player in a group drill who gets five minutes of attention per practice.

Too many young players get lost in big group settings. They go through the motions, they don't get real feedback, and they stop improving. Worse — they stop enjoying the game.

That's not how I coach. At Hardwood Academy, every session is designed around one player. Their strengths, their weak spots, their personality, their goals. We track progress every visit so both coach and player always know exactly where they stand.

Whether your child has never picked up a basketball or is preparing for a travel team tryout, I'm here to help them get to the next level — and love the game the whole way there.
