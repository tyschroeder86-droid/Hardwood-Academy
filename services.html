Pricing Philosophy

Why these
numbers?

Pricing was built around one goal: making elite coaching accessible to real families while ensuring every session delivers genuine value.

At $60/session, 1-on-1 training is priced significantly below larger training facilities and private coaches in the area — while offering the same (or better) level of personal attention. You get a dedicated coach for every minute of every session.

The Starter Package at $200 bundles four sessions for new families who want to try the program with commitment. At $50/session effectively, it's our best per-session rate — and comes with an assessment and drill guide included.

The Small Group rate at $30/player brings quality training within reach for families on tighter budgets, while keeping sessions small enough that every player still gets real coaching attention.

Common Questions

Before you book.

What age groups do you work with? ▾
I train kids ages 6 to 14. No prior experience needed — all skill levels are welcome from complete beginners to players looking to sharpen their varsity-level game.
Where do sessions take place? ▾
Sessions are held at local gyms and outdoor courts in the State College area. Exact location details are confirmed after booking based on availability and your preference.
What should my child bring? ▾
Athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a willingness to work hard. Coach Ty brings all balls and equipment — you just need to show up ready to go.
Is the first session really free? ▾
Yes — completely free, no commitment required. The first session is a chance for your player to experience the training style and for Coach Ty to assess where they are. You only pay if you decide to continue.
Can I upgrade from the Starter Package to regular sessions? ▾
Absolutely. Many players start with the Starter Package and transition directly into weekly 1-on-1 sessions. Just let Coach Ty know your goals after the assessment session.

Start with zero risk.

The first session is completely free. No sign-ups, no credit card, no commitment. Just bring your player out and see what Hardwood Academy can do for their game.
